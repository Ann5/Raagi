    RLDA Summer Camps Registrations Open for the year 2017

        Hurray! Congratulations to our Bollywood Team for winning first and "Classical Team" for winning second at "I Love Dance 2017". We're proud of you!!!

It's SUMMER!! Let’s learn something new with lots of fun and dance.
RLDA is all excited to offer "Summer Camp 2017"between 9AM and 4PM and offers dance, yoga, music, art, study time and language classes (both Telugu and Hindi). The camp will be filled with fun activities and focuses on overall development of the kid during holiday time. We have successfully offered "Summer Camp" for the last two years and have been greatly appreciated by parents and guardians for quality and learning methodologies.
This year, we are offering an intense 2-hour "Summer Dance Workshop" in the evenings for an additional small fee which focuses on next-level dance choreography preparing your kid to become a better performer!
